# Hotel Management System

## Overview

The Hotel Management System is a team project developed for the Introduction to Software Engineering course at the University of Science - VNUHCM.

The system was designed to optimize hotel operations and improve customer experience by supporting online room booking, payment processing, room management, customer service, and internal hotel management.

---

# Project Objectives

* Support customers in searching and booking rooms online.
* Improve hotel management efficiency.
* Manage hotel services and customer requests.
* Support employee and customer information management.
* Provide a better customer experience through online services.

# Features

## Customer Features

* Search available rooms
* Filter rooms by type, price, and status
* Online room booking
* Online room cancellation
* Booking history management
* Multiple payment methods
* Live chat support
* Customer reviews and feedback
* Discount coupon system

## Hotel Service Features

* Room cleaning requests
* Room repair requests
* Service management

## Hotel Management Features

* Employee management
* Customer management
* Work shift scheduling
* Salary calculation and employee ranking
* Room and service status management

# System Architecture

## Client Layer

Provides interfaces for:

* Room searching
* Room booking
* Payment processing
* Booking history
* Customer support chat
* Review submission

## Server Layer

Handles:

* Business logic
* Room management
* Employee scheduling
* Salary calculation
* Customer and employee management
* Promotion management

## Database Layer

Stores:

* Customer information
* Booking history
* Room and service information
* Employee information
* Payment transactions
* Discount coupons
